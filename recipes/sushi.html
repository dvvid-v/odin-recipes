Smoked Salmon Sushi Roll

This smoked salmon sushi roll recipe is the perfect option for a dinner night with friends, or even as a way to impress your significant other. Why go out and purchase what you can easily make at home! Homemade salmon sushi rolls have never been easier to make and this recipe will prove just that!

Ingredients

We will need the following ingredients to make it happen,

  • sushi rice
  • rice wine vinegar
  • seaweed
  • wasabi paste
  • smoked salmon
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 avocado

Steps

  1. Soak and cook the rice
  2. Mix in the cooked rice in the vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate.
  3. Assemble the rolls based on preference.
  4. Roll the sushi tightly and then finally cut into eight pieces.
